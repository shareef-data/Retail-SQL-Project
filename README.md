## 📌 Project Overview
This project simulates a **retail sales database** using MySQL.  
It demonstrates database design, data insertion, and advanced SQL queries to generate meaningful business insights.

The database includes structured tables for products, customers, orders, and order items to replicate a real-world retail system.

---

## 🗂️ Database Structure

### Tables Created:

- **Products** – Stores product details (ID, name, category, price)
- **Customers** – Stores customer information (ID, name, email, region, join date)
- **Orders** – Contains order-level information (Order ID, Customer ID, Order Date)
- **Order_Items** – Stores product-level order details (Order ID, Product ID, Quantity)

---

## 📈 Business Analysis Queries

This project answers important business questions such as:

1. Total revenue generated
2. Monthly revenue trends
3. Revenue by region
4. Top customers by revenue
5. One-time vs repeat customers
6. Revenue contribution by product

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Identifies best-performing regions
- Highlights top revenue-generating customers
- Analyzes monthly sales trends
- Evaluates customer retention
- Determines product contribution to overall revenue
